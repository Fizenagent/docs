Fizen takes your privacy seriously. This page explains what data we collect when you use our products, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have over your information.

<Warning>
  This page is a summary for your convenience. The full Privacy Policy is available at [fizen.io/privacy](https://fizen.io/privacy). The full policy governs in all cases.
</Warning>

## What we collect

When you create a Fizen account and use our products, we collect the following types of data:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account information">
    Your name, email address, phone number, and the credentials you use to log in. This information is required to create and maintain your account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Identity verification (KYC) documents">
    To issue you a Fizen Card, we are legally required to verify your identity. This includes government-issued ID documents and, in some cases, proof of address. KYC data is processed by regulated verification partners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction data">
    A record of the transactions you make with your Fizen Card, including amounts, merchant details, currencies, and timestamps. This data is retained for compliance and fraud prevention purposes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Device and usage data">
    Information about the device you use to access Fizen, including device type, operating system, IP address, and app usage patterns. We use this data to detect fraud, maintain security, and improve the product.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How we use your data

| Purpose | Data used |
|---|---|
| Identity verification and fraud prevention | KYC documents, account info, device data |
| Processing transactions | Account info, transaction data |
| Customer support | Account info, transaction history |
| Legal and regulatory compliance | All categories, as required |
| Product improvement | Anonymized usage data |

<Note>
  We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data with service providers only to the extent necessary to operate our products and comply with the law.
</Note>

  <Card title="Access" icon="eye">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Correction" icon="pen">

To exercise any of these rights, contact [info@fizen.io](mailto:info@fizen.io). We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

## Data security

Fizen uses industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. Despite these measures, no system is completely immune to risk. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact [support@fizen.io](mailto:support@fizen.io) immediately.

## Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for the period required by applicable financial regulations after account closure. KYC records are typically retained for a minimum of five years as required by anti-money-laundering laws.
