## Brand identity

Fizen's brand is built around trust, simplicity, and global utility. Our visual identity reflects a fintech product that is professional, accessible, and modern.

## Logo

<Info>
  For official logo files, contact [business@fizen.io](mailto:business@fizen.io) or download from the Fizen press kit.
</Info>

Use the Fizen logo as provided. Do not:
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the logo colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place the logo on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast

## Brand colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Fizen Blue | `#1a56db` | Primary — CTA buttons, links, accents |
| White | `#ffffff` | Backgrounds, text on dark |
| Dark | `#0f172a` | Body text, dark backgrounds |
| Light Gray | `#f8fafc` | Page backgrounds, cards |

## Typography

- **Display / Headings**: Clean sans-serif, weight 600–700
- **Body text**: Regular weight, 16px, line-height 1.6
- **UI labels**: Medium weight, 13–14px

## Voice & tone

- Direct and clear — no jargon, no crypto hype
- Practical — focus on real utility and specific numbers
- Trustworthy — backed by Tether, transparent fees
- Global — accessible to non-native English speakers

**Avoid**: "revolutionary", "next-gen", "unlimited", "guaranteed", "to the moon"

**Use**: specific numbers, real pain points, actionable language
