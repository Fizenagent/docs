Fizen's brand reflects trust, simplicity, and global utility. If you're a partner, press contact, or community member using Fizen's name or assets, these guidelines help you represent the brand accurately.

## Brand identity

Fizen is a crypto-powered payment platform built for travelers and people who live across borders. Our visual identity is professional, accessible, and modern — designed to convey reliability without complexity.

<Info>
  For official logo files and press kit assets, contact [business@fizen.io](mailto:business@fizen.io).
</Info>

## Logo

The Fizen logo is provided as the primary representation of our brand. Use it as supplied — do not alter it.

**Do not:**
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Change the logo colors
- Add effects such as shadows, gradients, or outlines
- Place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds

<Note>
  When in doubt about logo placement or sizing, contact [business@fizen.io](mailto:business@fizen.io) before publishing.
</Note>

## Brand colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Fizen Blue | `#1a56db` | Primary — CTA buttons, links, accents |
| White | `#ffffff` | Backgrounds, text on dark surfaces |
| Dark | `#0f172a` | Body text, dark backgrounds |
| Light Gray | `#f8fafc` | Page backgrounds, cards |

## Typography

- **Display and headings**: Clean sans-serif, weight 600–700
- **Body text**: Regular weight, 16px, line-height 1.6
- **UI labels**: Medium weight, 13–14px

## Voice and tone

Fizen's communication is direct, practical, and accessible. We write for a global audience that includes non-native English speakers, so clarity always comes first.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Direct and clear" icon="check">
    No jargon, no crypto hype. Say what you mean in plain language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Practical" icon="wrench">
    Focus on real utility and specific numbers. Vague promises don't help anyone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trustworthy" icon="shield-check">
    Back claims with facts. Fizen is backed by Tether — that's a specific, verifiable statement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Global" icon="globe">
    Write for someone in Jakarta, São Paulo, or Kyiv — not just native English speakers in the US.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Avoid:** "revolutionary", "next-gen", "unlimited", "guaranteed", "to the moon"

**Use:** specific numbers, real user pain points, actionable language
