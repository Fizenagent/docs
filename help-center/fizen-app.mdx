## What is the Fizen App?

The Fizen Super App is your all-in-one crypto neobank. Everything you need to live, spend, and manage crypto on the go — in a single app.

<img src="/images/app-overview.png" alt="Fizen App Overview" />

## What you can do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pay with QR">
    Scan any QR code to pay with USDT — especially useful across Southeast Asia and LATAM where QR payments are part of daily life.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Manage your Fizen Card">
    View balance, transaction history, top up, freeze/unfreeze your card — all from the app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Buy & sell crypto">
    Buy and sell crypto using bank transfer or card. Supports multiple fiat currencies.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Send money internationally">
    Send USDT across borders instantly. Lower cost than traditional wire transfers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Book travel">
    Book hotels, flights, and purchase global eSIM — paid with your crypto balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tokenized US stocks">
    Access tokenized US stocks and ETFs directly from your crypto balance inside the app.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
