The Fizen Super App is your all-in-one crypto neobank for iOS and Android. Whether you're paying at a street market in Bangkok, sending money home, or booking a flight with USDT, it's all here — no browser, no extra tools, no conversions.

<Note>
  Fizen is backed by Tether — the company behind USDT. Your funds stay on-chain and you remain in control.
</Note>

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Get the Fizen App on [iOS or Android](https://url.fizen.io/downloadgb). Create your account with your email or phone number — it takes less than 2 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYC verification">
    Submit a government-issued ID and take a selfie. Most verifications complete in under 5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add funds">
    Deposit USDT to your Fizen wallet from any external wallet or exchange.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Fizen">
    Pay with QR, apply for your Fizen Card, send money, or book travel — all from the home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features in detail

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pay with QR">
    Open the app and tap **Scan**. Point your camera at any QR code to pay with USDT instantly. This works at Fizen Pay merchants and any wallet that accepts USDT payments. QR payments are widely used across Southeast Asia and LATAM — markets where Fizen is built to operate.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fizen Card management">
    The **Card** tab gives you a full view of your prepaid Visa card. Check your available balance, browse your transaction history, and top up directly from your USDT wallet. If you ever need to pause your card, freeze it in one tap — and unfreeze it just as quickly when you're ready.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Buy and sell crypto">
    Tap **Buy** or **Sell** to convert between USDT and local fiat currency using a bank transfer or linked card. Rates are shown in real time before you confirm, so you always know what you're getting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="International money transfers">
    Send USDT to any wallet address worldwide. Transfers settle in seconds and cost a fraction of what traditional wire transfers charge. No bank account required on the receiving end.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Travel booking">
    Book hotels and flights directly inside the app using your USDT balance. You can also purchase a global eSIM for internet access in 100+ countries — no physical SIM swap needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tokenized US stocks">
    Invest in tokenized versions of US stocks and ETFs from inside the app. You don't need a brokerage account — just a Fizen wallet with a USDT balance. Positions are represented on-chain.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  You can use Fizen Card with Google Pay and Apple Pay — add it to your phone wallet and tap to pay anywhere Visa is accepted, even before your physical card arrives.
</Tip>
