Fizen Pay is a cryptocurrency payment gateway built for businesses. It lets you accept USDT from customers with no chargebacks, no intermediaries, and real-time settlement — whether you operate a physical store, an online platform, or both.

<Info>
  Fizen Pay is used by merchants across Southeast Asia and LATAM. Any business that wants to accept USDT can get started.
</Info>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your business">
    Sign up at [fizen.io](https://fizen.io) and complete business verification. Contact the team at [business@fizen.io](mailto:business@fizen.io) to start the onboarding process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your integration method">
    Use a static QR code at your counter for walk-in customers, generate dynamic QR codes per transaction via the dashboard, or integrate programmatically using the Fizen Pay API for online checkouts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer pays with USDT">
    Your customer scans the QR code with their Fizen App or any compatible USDT wallet and confirms the payment. The whole process takes seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive USDT instantly">
    Payment confirms in real time. USDT lands in your Fizen Pay balance with no waiting period and no third-party delay.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Integration options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="QR code payments" icon="qrcode">

## Why merchants choose Fizen Pay

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No chargebacks">
    Crypto payments are final. Once a customer pays, the transaction cannot be reversed. This eliminates chargeback fraud — a major cost for merchants accepting card payments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real-time settlement">
    USDT arrives in your Fizen Pay balance within seconds of the customer confirming payment. You don't wait for bank clearing windows or end-of-day settlement batches.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accept payments from anywhere">
    Any customer with a USDT wallet can pay you — regardless of their country, bank, or payment network. This is especially valuable for cross-border sales and markets where card infrastructure is limited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transaction dashboard">
    Your Fizen Pay dashboard gives you a full view of all incoming payments, transaction history, and balances. Export records for accounting or connect via API to sync with your existing systems.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Simple for customers">
    Customers pay with the Fizen App or any USDT-compatible wallet — no new account needed on their end. Scanning a QR code and confirming is all it takes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Fizen Pay currently supports USDT payments only. Payments in other cryptocurrencies are not accepted through this gateway.
</Warning>

## Who Fizen Pay is for

- Physical retailers in Southeast Asia and LATAM who already have customers paying with crypto
- Online platforms that want to offer USDT as a payment option at checkout
- Businesses that operate in markets where card payments are unreliable or costly
- Merchants who want to eliminate chargeback risk entirely

<Tip>
  If you run a physical store, you can get started with just a printed QR code — no technical setup required. The API option is available when you're ready to automate.
</Tip>
