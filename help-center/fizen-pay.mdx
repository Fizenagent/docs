## What is Fizen Pay?

Fizen Pay is a cryptocurrency payment gateway for businesses. Accept USDT payments from customers — at your physical store via QR code, or online via API integration.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for Fizen Pay">
    Register your business account at [fizen.io](https://fizen.io) and complete business verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your payment QR">
    Create a static QR for your counter or generate dynamic QR codes per transaction via the dashboard or API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer pays with USDT">
    Customers scan the QR with their Fizen App or any compatible USDT wallet and confirm payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive USDT instantly">
    Payment confirms in seconds. USDT lands in your Fizen Pay balance — no waiting, no intermediaries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why use Fizen Pay?

- No chargebacks — crypto payments are final
- Accept payments from customers globally
- Real-time settlement in USDT
- Dashboard to track all transactions
- API available for online integration
