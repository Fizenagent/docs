## What is the Fizen Card?

The Fizen Card is a Visa prepaid card that lets you load USDT and spend it like cash, anywhere in the world. You load USDT from your Fizen wallet, and the card handles everything else — currency conversion, contactless payments, ATM withdrawals, and online purchases — exactly like a regular Visa card.

No bank account required. No complex conversions before you pay.

<Note>
  The Fizen Card is backed by Tether, the company behind USDT, giving you a direct bridge from your crypto holdings to everyday spending.
</Note>

## Who the Fizen Card is for

The Fizen Card is a strong fit if any of these apply to you:

- You hold USDT and are tired of paying 2–5% to spend it through P2P exchanges
- You travel in Southeast Asia and want a single card that works everywhere
- You run ad spend on Meta, Google, or TikTok and need a reliable funding method
- You are a freelancer paid in USDT and want to stop losing money on every conversion
- You are from a country where most fintech providers do not accept your documents
