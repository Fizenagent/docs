## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Fizen App">
    Get the app on [iOS or Android](https://url.fizen.io/downloadgb). Create your account with your email or phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYC verification">
    Submit a valid government ID and take a selfie. Verification usually takes under 5 minutes — some cases up to 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply for Fizen Card">
    Go to the **Card** section in the app → tap **Get Card**. Choose virtual or physical card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your virtual card instantly">
    Your virtual card details (card number, expiry, CVV) are available immediately after approval. Use it online right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive physical card (optional)">
    Physical card ships in 7–14 business days to your registered address.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Eligible documents

| Nationality | Accepted documents |
|---|---|
| Most countries | Passport, National ID |
| Russian nationals | Russian passport (eligible) |
| Chinese nationals | Chinese passport (eligible) |

<Info>
  Card availability depends on your country of residence. Contact support if you have questions about eligibility.
</Info>
