## Before you apply

You need a Fizen account to apply for a Fizen Card. If you have not created one yet, download the Fizen App first.

<Info>
  Card availability depends on your country of residence. If you are unsure whether you are eligible, [contact support](/information/contact) before you begin.
</Info>

## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Fizen App">
    Install the Fizen App on [iOS or Android](https://url.fizen.io/downloadgb) and create your account using your email address or phone number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete identity verification (KYC)">
    Submit a valid government-issued ID and take a selfie for facial verification. Verification typically completes in under 5 minutes. Complex cases may take up to 24 hours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Card section">
    Open the Fizen App, tap **Card** in the bottom navigation, then tap **Get Card**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose virtual or physical card">
    Select the card type you want. You can apply for a virtual card, a physical card, or both.

    - **Virtual card** — issued instantly after approval; use it online right away
    - **Physical card** — ships to your registered address in 7–14 business days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start spending">
    Once your virtual card is issued, top it up with USDT and start spending. Your card number, expiry date, and CVV are available immediately in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Accepted identity documents

| Nationality | Accepted documents |
|---|---|
| Most countries | Passport, National ID |
| Russian nationals | Russian passport |
| Chinese nationals | Chinese passport |

<Warning>
  Your document must be valid (not expired) and the photo must be clearly readable. Blurry or cropped images will delay your verification.
</Warning>
