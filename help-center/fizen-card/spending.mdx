## How your card works

Your Fizen Card is a Visa prepaid card. It works at any merchant or terminal that accepts Visa — over 150 million worldwide. There is nothing special to configure. Load USDT, and spend.

## Payment methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tap to Pay (NFC)" icon="wifi">
    Hold your physical card against any NFC-enabled Visa terminal to pay contactlessly. No PIN required for low-value transactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chip & PIN" icon="microchip">
    Insert your physical card into the terminal and enter your PIN. Works at any standard POS terminal that accepts Visa.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online payments" icon="globe">
    Enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV at checkout. Your virtual card details are available in the app immediately after issuance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Magstripe" icon="credit-card">
    Swipe your physical card at terminals that still use the magnetic stripe reader. Supported as a fallback where NFC and chip are not available.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Pay" icon="google">
    Add your Fizen Card to Google Pay and tap to pay with your Android phone or watch at any contactless terminal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apple Pay" icon="apple">
    Add your Fizen Card to Apple Pay and pay with your iPhone or Apple Watch anywhere contactless Visa is accepted.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## ATM withdrawals

You can withdraw cash from any Visa-compatible ATM worldwide.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Visa ATM">
    Look for the Visa logo on the ATM. Most ATMs worldwide support Visa cards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert your card and enter your PIN">
    Use the PIN you set in the Fizen App. If you have not set a PIN yet, go to **Card → Settings → Set PIN** in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your withdrawal amount">
    Enter the amount in local currency. The equivalent USDT will be deducted from your card balance at the current exchange rate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  ATM withdrawals incur a fee. See the [fee schedule](/help-center/fizen-card/fees) for current ATM fees before withdrawing.
</Info>

## Currency conversion

When you spend in a currency other than USD, Fizen applies the real-time Visa exchange rate at the moment of the transaction. Fizen does not add a markup on top of the Visa rate — any applicable foreign currency fee is listed separately in the [fee schedule](/help-center/fizen-card/fees).

| Spend currency | How it works |
|---|---|
| USD | Deducted 1:1 from your card balance — no conversion needed |
| Any other currency | Converted at the real-time Visa FX rate at transaction time |

## Transaction limits

Your limits depend on your card tier. You can check your current limits at any time in the Fizen App under **Card → Limits**.

| Limit type | Details |
|---|---|
| Daily spending | Depends on your card tier |
| Single transaction | Depends on your card tier |
| ATM withdrawal | Supported — daily limit applies |
| Online transactions | Within your daily spend limit |

<Tip>
  If a transaction is declined, check your card balance and daily limits in the app first before contacting support. Most declines are due to insufficient balance or an exceeded limit.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My card was declined">
    Check the following in the Fizen App:
    - Your card balance is sufficient for the transaction amount
    - You have not exceeded your daily spend limit
    - Your card is active (not frozen or expired)

    If everything looks correct, [contact support](/information/contact).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't know my card PIN">
    Go to **Card → Settings → Set PIN** in the Fizen App to set or reset your PIN. The change takes effect immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want to freeze or unfreeze my card">
    Go to **Card → Settings → Freeze Card** in the Fizen App. You can freeze and unfreeze your card instantly at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="An ATM didn't give me cash but my balance was deducted">
    This can happen when an ATM times out mid-transaction. The hold typically reverses within 1–5 business days. If it does not, [contact support](/information/contact) with the date, ATM location, and amount.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
