## How to spend

Your Fizen Card works like any Visa card — use it anywhere Visa is accepted.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Tap to Pay" icon="wifi">
    Use NFC contactless at any terminal that accepts Visa contactless payments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chip & PIN" icon="microchip">
    Insert your physical card and enter your PIN at POS terminals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online payments" icon="globe">
    Use your card number, expiry, and CVV for any online purchase.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Pay & Apple Pay" icon="mobile">
    Add your Fizen Card to your phone wallet and tap to pay.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Currency conversion

When you spend in a currency other than USD, Fizen uses the real-time Visa exchange rate. No hidden FX markup from Fizen's side — check the fee schedule for any applicable conversion fees.

## Limits

| Type | Limit |
|---|---|
| Daily spend | Depends on your card tier |
| ATM withdrawal | Supported (fees apply) |
| Online transactions | Unlimited within daily limit |
