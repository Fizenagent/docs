## Fee schedule

<Info>
  Fizen is committed to transparent pricing. All fees are shown in the app before you confirm any transaction.
</Info>

## Card fees

| Fee type | Amount |
|---|---|
| Card issuance (virtual) | Free |
| Card issuance (physical) | See app for current pricing |
| Monthly maintenance | Free |
| Top-up fee | Free |

## Spending fees

| Fee type | Amount |
|---|---|
| USD spending | Free |
| Foreign currency spending | Real-time Visa FX rate applies |
| Online transactions | Free |
| Contactless / Chip & PIN | Free |

## ATM fees

| Fee type | Amount |
|---|---|
| ATM withdrawal | Fee applies — see app |
| ATM balance inquiry | Fee applies |

## Other fees

| Fee type | Amount |
|---|---|
| Card replacement | See app |
| Inactivity fee | None |
| Account closure | None |

<Warning>
  Fees are subject to change. Always check the latest fee schedule in the Fizen App under Card → Fee Schedule.
</Warning>
