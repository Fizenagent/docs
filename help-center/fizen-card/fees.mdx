<Info>
  All fees are displayed in the Fizen App before you confirm any transaction. You will never be surprised by a charge you did not see coming.
</Info>

## Card issuance fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Virtual card issuance | Free |
| Physical card issuance | See the Fizen App for current pricing |
| Monthly maintenance | Free |
| Inactivity fee | None |

## Top-up fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Top-up from Fizen wallet (USDT) | Free |
| Top-up via external USDT transfer (TRC20 / ERC20) | Free |

<Note>
  Network gas fees for external transfers are paid to the blockchain network, not to Fizen. These vary depending on network congestion and are not controlled by Fizen.
</Note>

## Spending fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| USD purchases | Free |
| Foreign currency purchases | Real-time Visa FX rate — no Fizen markup |
| Online transactions | Free |
| Contactless payments (NFC / Tap to Pay) | Free |
| Chip & PIN payments | Free |
| Magstripe payments | Free |

## ATM fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| ATM cash withdrawal | Fee applies — check the Fizen App |
| ATM balance inquiry | Fee applies — check the Fizen App |

<Warning>
  ATM operators may also charge their own fees on top of Fizen's ATM fee. These third-party charges are set by the ATM operator and are not shown in the Fizen App.
</Warning>

## Other fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Card replacement (lost or damaged) | See the Fizen App |
| Account closure | Free |

## Spending limits

Your spending limits depend on your card tier. Check the Fizen App under **Card → Limits** for your current tier's limits.

| Limit type | Details |
|---|---|
| Daily spend | Depends on card tier |
| Single transaction | Depends on card tier |
| ATM withdrawal (daily) | Depends on card tier |
| Cards per account | Up to 200 |
| Minimum top-up | $10 USDT |
| Maximum top-up | No limit |

<Tip>
  To see the exact limits for your account right now, open the Fizen App and go to **Card → Limits**. Limits may increase as your account history grows.
</Tip>

## Fee changes

<Warning>
  Fees are subject to change. The fee schedule in the Fizen App is always the most up-to-date version. Go to **Card → Fee Schedule** in the app to see current fees before any transaction.
</Warning>
