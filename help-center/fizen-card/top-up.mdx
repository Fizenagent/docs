## How to top up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Fizen App">
    Go to your **Card** dashboard and tap **Top Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose amount">
    Enter the USDT amount you want to load. Minimum top-up is **$10 USDT**. No maximum limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review and confirm the transaction. Your card balance updates instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Top-up methods

| Method | Speed | Min amount |
|---|---|---|
| From Fizen wallet (USDT) | Instant | $10 |
| External USDT transfer (TRC20/ERC20) | 1–3 min | $10 |

<Warning>
  Always send USDT on the correct network (TRC20 or ERC20). Sending on wrong network may result in permanent loss of funds.
</Warning>

## Tips

- Your USDT balance and card balance are separate inside the app
- Top up as needed — no need to load large amounts upfront
- Card balance shows in USD equivalent at time of load
