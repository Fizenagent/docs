## How to top up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Fizen App">
    Go to the **Card** section in the app and tap **Top Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    Type in the USDT amount you want to load. The minimum top-up is **$10 USDT**. There is no maximum top-up limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Review the amount and tap **Confirm**. Your card balance updates instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Your card balance shows in USD equivalent at the time you load. Top up in smaller amounts as you need them — there is no requirement to load a large balance upfront.
</Tip>

## Top-up methods

| Method | Speed | Minimum amount |
|---|---|---|
| From your Fizen wallet (USDT) | Instant | $10 USDT |
| External USDT transfer via TRC20 | 1–3 minutes | $10 USDT |
| External USDT transfer via ERC20 | 1–3 minutes | $10 USDT |

<Warning>
  Always send USDT on the correct network. Sending funds on the wrong network (for example, sending ERC20 USDT to a TRC20 address) can result in permanent loss of funds. Double-check the network before confirming any external transfer.
</Warning>

## Things to know

- Your **Fizen wallet balance** and your **card balance** are separate inside the app. A top-up moves funds from your wallet to your card.
- Card balance is denominated in USD equivalent at the time of the top-up. Exchange rate fluctuations after loading do not affect your card balance.
- There is no fee to top up — see the [full fee schedule](/help-center/fizen-card/fees) for all other charges.
