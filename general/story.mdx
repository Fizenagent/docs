## Our mission

**Our mission is simple: to make the lives of hundreds of millions of people better with crypto.**

My name is Leo Vu — CEO and Co-founder of Fizen.

I'm not a polished writer, and I don't try to be. What I do have is conviction. Everything here is written directly by me — no fancy words, just honesty and the belief that what we're building matters.

## Why Fizen exists

Crypto has been around for over a decade, yet most people still can't use it to pay for a coffee, book a hotel, or send money home to family. The infrastructure has lagged far behind the promise.

We started Fizen because we lived this problem. Traveling across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Eastern Europe, we saw firsthand how broken cross-border payments are — high fees, slow transfers, and banking systems that exclude hundreds of millions of people.

We asked a simple question: what if you could spend crypto anywhere Visa is accepted, in any currency, instantly?

That question became Fizen.

## What drives us

Fizen is driven by a team that shows up every day with the same motivation: to create products people actually use, trust, and rely on. That's the only standard we care about.

## Where we're headed

We're expanding across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Eastern Europe — regions where people need better financial tools the most. Every new market we enter, every card we issue, and every transaction we process brings us closer to that mission.

<Note>
  Fizen is not a bank. We're a crypto-powered payment platform built on Visa's network. Your card balance is managed in crypto and converted at the point of sale.
</Note>
