Whether you're paying for dinner, running ad campaigns, or accepting crypto at your store, Fizen has a product designed for that moment. All three work together inside the Fizen App and are powered by USDT.

## The three products

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Fizen Super App" icon="mobile-screen" href="/help-center/fizen-app">
    Your command center for everything Fizen. Buy, sell, and send crypto, pay merchants via QR code, book flights and hotels, and manage your Fizen Card — all from a single app on iOS or Android.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fizen Card" icon="credit-card" href="/help-center/fizen-card/overview">
    A USDT-powered Visa prepaid card that works at 150M+ merchants worldwide. Tap to pay in stores, shop online, add to Google Pay or Apple Pay, and issue up to 200 cards from one account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fizen Pay" icon="store" href="/help-center/fizen-pay">
    A cryptocurrency payment gateway for businesses. Display a QR code at your counter or embed a checkout flow online — your customers pay in USDT and you receive funds instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the products work together

Each product is useful on its own, but they're designed to complement each other:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="From wallet to card in seconds" icon="wallet">
    Hold USDT in the Fizen App, then top up your Fizen Card with a few taps. There's no bank transfer, no waiting period — the balance is available immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pay anywhere Visa is accepted" icon="credit-card">
    Once your card is loaded, you can spend at any of the 150M+ Visa-accepting merchants worldwide — restaurants, hotels, online stores, ad platforms, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accept crypto at your business" icon="store">
    If you run a business, Fizen Pay lets you accept USDT from customers without requiring them to have a Fizen account. You control where the funds go.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
