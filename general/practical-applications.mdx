## Real-world scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Digital nomad in Southeast Asia" icon="plane">
    You're working remotely across Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia. Instead of carrying local cash or paying ATM fees, you use your Fizen Card to tap at restaurants, pay hotel bills, and buy groceries. Your USDT balance stays intact — you only spend what you load, at no extra cost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Media buyer running ad campaigns" icon="bullseye-arrow">
    You manage Facebook and Google ad accounts and need a reliable USD card that isn't blocked by local banking restrictions. You load USDT into the Fizen App, top up a Fizen Card, and use it for ad spend. With up to 200 cards per account, you can separate budgets across clients or campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Freelancer receiving USDT payments" icon="laptop">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Business owner accepting crypto" icon="store">

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Fizen App">
    Get the app on iOS or Android. Create your account with an email or phone number in under two minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete identity verification">
    Submit a government-issued ID and a selfie. Most verifications finish in under five minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply for your Fizen Card">
    Go to the **Card** section and tap **Get Card**. Your virtual card is issued instantly and ready to use online straight away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up and spend">
    Transfer USDT to your card balance and spend anywhere Visa is accepted — in store, online, or via Google Pay and Apple Pay.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you're a business, head to [Fizen Pay](/help-center/fizen-pay) to start accepting USDT from customers without requiring them to have a Fizen account.
</Tip>
