## What this means for you

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why does Tether's backing matter?">
    Tether is the issuer of USDT — the most widely used stablecoin in crypto. Their backing means Fizen has both the financial resources and the stablecoin infrastructure to support your card balance at scale. When you top up your Fizen Card with USDT, it's settled on infrastructure built and trusted by the team that created it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where can I use my Fizen Card?">
    Anywhere Visa is accepted — that's over 150 million merchants across more than 200 countries. This includes online stores, physical retailers, restaurants, hotels, and ATMs. You can use your card for everyday spending wherever you are in the world.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is Fizen built for?">
    Fizen is designed for digital nomads, frequent travelers, remote workers, and anyone who earns, saves, or spends across borders. If you hold crypto and want to use it in the real world without friction, Fizen is built for you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What regions does Fizen support?">
    Fizen is actively growing across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Eastern Europe — regions where traditional banking is expensive, slow, or inaccessible. We continue to expand to new markets. Check the Fizen app for the most current list of supported countries.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
