## Affiliate program

The Fizen affiliate program rewards you for introducing new users to the Fizen Card. When someone you refer activates a card and transacts, you earn a commission.

- **Tiered structure** — your commission rate increases as your referral volume grows, from Nano to Pro affiliate tier
- **Trackable links** — you receive a unique referral link to share across any channel
- **Real-time dashboard** — monitor your referrals, activations, and earnings

<Tip>
  The affiliate program works well for content creators, crypto community managers, travel bloggers, and anyone with an audience that spends internationally.
</Tip>

## Distribution partnership

As a distribution partner, you can integrate Fizen Card into your own platform or offer it to your community under a co-branded arrangement. This is suited for:

- **Fintech and neobank platforms** looking to add a crypto spending card
- **Travel apps and booking platforms** whose users need a borderless payment method
- **Crypto projects and DAOs** wanting to give members a real-world spending option

<Note>
  Distribution partnerships are evaluated individually. Reach out with your platform details and we'll discuss what's possible.
</Note>

## Community and co-marketing

Fizen collaborates with communities and media partners to grow together. If you run a Telegram group, YouTube channel, podcast, or crypto event, there may be a fit. Co-marketing opportunities include:

- Sponsored content and product reviews
- Joint campaigns and giveaways
- Event sponsorship and presence
- Cross-promotion to Fizen's global user base
