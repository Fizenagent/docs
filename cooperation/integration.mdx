## Fizen Pay Integration

Fizen Pay allows businesses to accept USDT payments seamlessly — via QR code at physical locations or through API integration for online platforms.

<Info>
  For integration inquiries, contact us at [business@fizen.io](mailto:business@fizen.io)
</Info>

## Integration options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="QR Payment" icon="qrcode">
    Generate a static or dynamic QR code. Customers scan and pay with USDT from any compatible wallet.
  </Card>
  <Card title="API Integration" icon="code">
    Connect Fizen Pay to your platform via API. Accept USDT payments programmatically with real-time confirmation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
