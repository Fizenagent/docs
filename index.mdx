## Get started in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Fizen App">
    Get the app on [iOS or Android](https://url.fizen.io/downloadgb). Create your account with your email or phone number — it takes less than 2 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYC verification">
    Submit a government-issued ID and a selfie. Most verifications complete in under 5 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your Fizen Card">
    Navigate to the **Card** section and tap **Get Card**. Your virtual card is issued instantly — use it online right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top up and spend">
    Transfer USDT to your card balance and start spending anywhere Visa is accepted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
