## Fizen is the crypto neobank built for travelers and global citizens.

Fizen simplifies life on the move. Whether you're exploring Southeast Asia, living abroad, or working remotely, Fizen helps you pay like a local, travel smoothly, send money across borders, and manage your crypto — all in one simple app.

Beyond everyday payments, Fizen is designed to make better use of your crypto. While you travel, spend, or live globally, your idle balance can stay productive in the background.
